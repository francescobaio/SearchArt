Labelling & Searching

What are Labelling and Searching and how to do

The Reason Why Labeling

A good label gives accurate information about the artwork (title, artist, date created, medium) but can also tell us a little bit about the artist and their process and or/ motivation to create. That additional information helps us understand, appreciate, and enjoy the work even more.

How to use

Use the button to the right to upload your collection and the application will find the objects inside any artwork.
At that point you will see that the size of many words in the TagCloud below will change meanwhile others will disappear.
A word with a big size means that the object related is very frequent inside your collection. When the progress bar ends you're ready to click one of these words and see what artworks have that object inside.
